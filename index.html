This is a simulation for the Sieve of Eratosthenes, a common prime number finding sieve.

The sieve is used to find the primes between 1 and any number N. To start, type in the number N into the input box.

Prime numbers are marked in orange and all other numbers are marked in red at the end of the simulation

To slow the simulation down, press the left arrow key. To speed it up, press the right arrow key.
